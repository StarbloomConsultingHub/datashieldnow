Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

What We Collect

When you run a scan, we collect your name and email address. This information is used exclusively to perform the data broker scan and deliver your results.

What We Do Not Do

  • We will never sell your personal information.
  • We will never share your data with third parties for marketing.
  • We will never use your scan data for anything other than your scan results.

Data Retention

Your name and email are deleted from our systems within 30 days of your scan unless you subscribe to a paid plan. Subscribers' data is retained only as long as the subscription is active and is deleted within 30 days of cancellation.

Third-Party Services

We use Stripe for payment processing. Stripe handles your payment information; we never see or store credit card numbers. We use Brevo for email delivery. Your email address is shared with Brevo only for the purpose of sending scan results and service emails.

Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies for site functionality. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics that share data with data brokers.

Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@datashieldnow.com. We will confirm deletion within 72 hours.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@datashieldnow.com.
